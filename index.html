POETRY

Peace

White with silver lining
love in heart
care in soul
honesty in core
empathy in being
harmony with all

Blue with copper drape
ocean in day and night
sky unlimited

Green with golden haze
nature in blossom

With all this
peace is before us
and the universe blessed.

Doha, December 2012
